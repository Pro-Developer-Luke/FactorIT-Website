Sustainable IT

More than symbolic — it's a commitment.

At FactorIT, we recognise the profound impact of climate change on our planet and its inhabitants. Our choice to feature the polar bear in our branding is more than symbolic; it's a commitment to awareness and action.

The climate crisis affects temperature regulation, rising sea levels, food systems, navigational hazards, wildlife habitats and permafrost stability. As a business, we believe in leveraging technology not only for business growth but also for fostering a sustainable future.

FactorIT supports organisations like the WWF, and weaves sustainability through everything we do — from responsibly sourced hardware to extended device lifecycles and minimised e-waste.
