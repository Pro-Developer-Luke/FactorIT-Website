What's Included in Your Free Assessment?

Four detailed checks covering your network, your team, your domain and your data.

  1. 01

    Network Assessment Tool

    Experience our non-intrusive RMM agent software, installed on your system to monitor performance and configuration for a week. Post-evaluation, we present you with a detailed report and a network improvement plan, benchmarked against industry standards, to enhance your IT infrastructure.

  2. 02

    Cyber Essentials Audit Questionnaire

    Engage with the Cyber Essentials framework through a questionnaire designed to assess your cyber-security posture. This self-assessment tool covers the five essential technical controls required for Cyber Essentials certification, reflecting your commitment to safeguarding data against common cyber threats.

  3. 03

    Deep Web Scan Report

    Undergo a thorough deep web scan of your company domain to identify any potential exposure of sensitive information, such as passwords or personal details, on the dark web. We provide a detailed report and advise on measures to protect your data from further breaches.

  4. 04

    E-mail & Domain Security Review

    Ensure your domain's email security adheres to best practices with our comprehensive review — including SPF record validation, DMARC, DKIM, and a domain web vulnerability scan.
